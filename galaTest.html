SATURDAY JUNE 8, 2013
6:00 P.M.

The name's Shedd – John G. Shedd. The 2013 annual black tie Gala brings a 007-sophisticated swagger inside the aquarium walls this year as Chicago's most prominent philanthropic leaders collude for an evening of cocktails, dinner and dancing that leave guests shakin' (not stirred). Be Like Bond: The Stingray Affair will feature an action-packed line-up of entertainment, including a unique, Bond-themed aquatic show, live auction, dinner among the fishes and dancing on Shedd's lakeside terraces. Your mission is simple – enjoy a night of cocktails, mystery, dinner and dancing as you show your support for Shedd and promote the future success of our work. This event will benefit five important aspects of the aquarium: animal care, conservation, education & learning, history & infrastructure, and community access.
