The Be Like Bond: Stingray Affair

SATURDAY JUNE, 8 2013
6:00 P.M. to Midnight

John G. Shedd Aquarium's annual black tie Gala is attended by approximately 850 of Chicago's philanthropic leaders for a night of cocktails, dinner, entertainment and dancing. Our much anticipated event is entitled Be Like Bond: The Stingray Affair and will feature an action-packed, cinematic experience for you and your guests to enjoy.

SCHEDULE

  • 6:00 P.M. — Shaken Not Stirred Sip martinis and stroll through the Abbott Oceanarium, but beware of spy submarines. Shedd photographers will capture your image with their Goldeneye.
  • 7:15 P.M. — For Your Eyes Only Be inspired by the aquatic show and say yes to Dr. No when he auctions spectacular prizes.
  • 8:00 P.M. — Live And Let Dine Savor a sumptuous dinner among the animals and enjoy the company of The Spy Who Loved Me.
  • 9:00 P.M. — Moonraker Beware of enemy agents attempting to break your rhythm while you dance until The Living Daylights appear.
  • Watch the Skyfall during the fireworks spectacular, then slip away to Q's Lab, sponsored by Dover. Enjoy cocktails and desserts served by Honey Ryder and Strawberry Fields. Gadgets optional.
